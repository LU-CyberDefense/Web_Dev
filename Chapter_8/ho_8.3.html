Dictator A satirical comedy following a ruthless North African leader who travels to the United States, where his identity is stolen. The film humorously explores politics, power, and freedom while showing the dictator’s unexpected journey toward humility.
Borat This mockumentary-style comedy features Borat, a journalist from Kazakhstan, as he travels across America to learn about its culture. Filled with outrageous humor and social commentary, it cleverly exposes stereotypes and cultural misunderstandings.
Avengers: Endgame An epic superhero film where the remaining Avengers unite for one final mission to undo the devastating effects of Thanos’s snap. It’s a powerful story of sacrifice, teamwork, and closure that concludes over a decade of Marvel storytelling.
